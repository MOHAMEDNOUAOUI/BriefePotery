About

Created in 1990, the National Ceramics Museum was taken over by the National Foundation of Museums in 2018 and is today among the renowned Moroccan museums thanks to to the richness and diversity of its collection consisting mainly of pottery and ceramics from Morocco. More than 600 objects are preserved and studied there, * and for the most part presented to the public.

ANIBA

POTERY ARTIST FROM SAFI

In the heart of our town, theres a gifted artisan whose hands bring clay to life. Our local potter, a master of the craft, sculpts ordinary clay into extraordinary pieces. Each creation is a blend of tradition and innovation, showcasing the potters skill and passion. Visit the workshop and you'll witness the magic firsthand the shaping of dreams the earthy scent of clay, and the artistry that turns mud into timeless treasures. Experience the beauty of handmade pottery, where every piece tells a story, and every shape holds a secret.
